Selected case studies

Turning planning, optimization and data discipline into measurable outcomes.

Jewelry · Domestic performance

Stable scaling toward VND 400M monthly spend

Challenge

Increase media volume without allowing acquisition costs to rise or weakening product sell-through.

Approach

Rebalanced reach, engagement, traffic and conversion campaigns using saturation, creative performance, response quality and sales conversion data.

Result: 3.0–4.25× more new-customer inquiries per comparable budget, conversion doubled to ~40%, and estimated cost per converted customer fell 83–88%.
FMCG · Annual planning

From VND 3.3B to VND 4.5B with a clearer channel mix

Challenge

Expand brand and product coverage while protecting domestic efficiency and supporting overseas market development.

Approach

Compared prior-year actuals, rebuilt KPI assumptions, removed low-value follower objectives, rebalanced channel weights and added market-specific awareness activity.

Result: A repeatable annual planning model with explicit KPI, budget, variance and brand/product allocation logic across Meta, Google, YouTube, TikTok and Zalo.
Creator media · 2026 plan

KOL/KOC booking and Code Ads as a separate growth engine

Challenge

Increase brand love and product reach without hiding creator investment inside the always-on platform budget.

Approach

Designed a dedicated creator-coverage lane, prioritized products by brand direction, and planned reach-focused TikTok Code Ads to amplify content on creator channels.

Result: VND 362.5M proposed as a distinct 2026 TikTok Code Ads pool, improving transparency, pacing control and cross-brand coverage planning.
